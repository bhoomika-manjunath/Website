About

FORCE SumMIT 2.O is a platform where ideas rise higher than skylines and ambition stands stronger than steel. It is not just a fest — it is a movement of innovation, intellect, and unstoppable energy. Step into a battleground of creativity, strategy, and skill. Challenge your limits.

Beyond competition, this is a space to connect, collaborate, and create lasting impact. It brings together diverse perspectives under one roof, fostering meaningful exchanges and unforgettable experiences that extend far beyond the summit days.

The Department of Civil Engineering, MIT Mysore, proudly presents a grand stage where future engineers rise, leaders emerge, and excellence takes shape. An extraordinary state-level celebration of talent, vision, and next-generation engineering.
